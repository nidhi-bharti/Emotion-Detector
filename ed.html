Abstract
The project aims at detecting human emotion expressed in an audio clip via speech recognition using four vital characteristic features of a speech signal. These are MFCC, VAD, pitch,and formants. The proposed methodology calculates the mean of features for each of the three emotions, namely, “JOY”,”ANGER” and “NEUTRALl” ; and uses the 2-Norm distance to predict emotion expressed . The algorithm is trained and tested using Berlin emotion Database.
1. Introduction
Estimation of human emotions using a computer has been difficult since when human engaged in a conversational secession. Humans encode their emotions while communicating. Different human speech signals containing the same emotion may vary from person to person.
